LGHS Open-Source Club

We're a group of students that meet every Monday and Friday on even weeks, and every Tuesday and Thursday on odd weeks. We do programming related projects together.

Our GitHub and Discord are linked below.


Projects

LGHS Chess (click on the knight!) is a vote chess web app that allows two schools to play chess against each other. The entire student body is able to participate and vote on the moves individually.
